## 2. Why you might “not see anything”

- The **home page** of the repo (e.g. `/RoyalWebsite`) shows **main** by default.  
  Your changes are on **RW-61**, so you won’t see them until you:
  - Open the **branch** dropdown (top left, next to the repo name) and choose **RW-61**, or  
  - Use the links above.

- **main** will only show your changes after you **merge** RW-61 into main (via a Pull Request or a local merge and push).
